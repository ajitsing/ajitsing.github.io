This privacy policy explains how **singhajit.com** ("this site", "we") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit. This site is a personal technical blog and does not sell products or services. Your privacy matters and this policy is written in plain language so you know exactly what happens when you visit.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you interact with this site, you may voluntarily provide information:

- **Comments**: If you leave a comment via GitHub (Utterances), your GitHub username and comment content are stored on GitHub. We do not control GitHub's data handling. See [GitHub's Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).
- **Contact**: If you email us directly, we receive your email address and message content.

### Information Collected Automatically

When you visit this site, certain information is collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies:

- **Analytics data**: Pages visited, time spent, referral source, browser type, device type, operating system, screen resolution, and approximate geographic location (country/city level)
- **Ad interaction data**: Ad impressions and clicks
- **Session recordings**: Mouse movements, clicks, scroll behavior, and page interactions (anonymized, with no personal data captured)

---

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This site uses cookies and similar technologies from the following services:

### Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (gtag.js) to understand how visitors use this site. Google Analytics collects:

- Pages you visit and how long you stay
- How you found this site (search engine, direct, referral)
- Your browser, device, and approximate location
- Interaction events (clicks, scrolls)

Google Analytics uses first-party cookies (prefixed `_ga`) to distinguish users. Data is aggregated and we cannot identify you personally.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### Google AdSense

This site displays advertisements through Google AdSense to support free content. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other sites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your browsing patterns.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/) or [aboutads.info](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

For more information, see [How Google Uses Information from Sites That Use Its Services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

### Microsoft Clarity

We use Microsoft Clarity for session recording and heatmaps. Clarity captures how you interact with pages (mouse movements, clicks, scroll depth) to help us improve usability. Clarity does not capture passwords, payment details, or other sensitive form inputs. Session data is anonymized.

For more information, see [Microsoft's Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement).

### Google Funding Choices

This site uses Google Funding Choices to detect ad blockers and display a consent message. This service may set cookies related to your consent preferences.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Understand readership**: Know which articles are helpful and which topics to write about
- **Improve the site**: Fix usability issues, optimize page load times, and improve navigation
- **Support the site**: Display relevant advertisements to fund hosting and content creation
- **Respond to you**: Reply to comments or emails you send

We do **not**:

- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Use your data for email marketing or newsletters (unless you explicitly subscribe)
- Build personal profiles for targeted outreach
- Share your data with third parties beyond the services listed above

## Data Retention

- **Google Analytics data** is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted
- **Microsoft Clarity data** is retained for up to 30 days
- **Comments** are stored on GitHub indefinitely unless you delete them from the GitHub issue
- **Server logs** are managed by the hosting provider and are typically retained for a limited period

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Delete** your data (for comments, you can delete them directly on GitHub)
- **Opt out** of analytics tracking and personalized ads using the links provided above
- **Disable cookies** through your browser settings

Since this is a personal blog with no user accounts or stored personal data beyond what third-party services collect, most data control is handled directly through those services.

### For EU/EEA Visitors (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation including the right to access, rectify, port, and erase your data. The legal basis for processing is legitimate interest (site analytics and improvement) and consent (personalized advertising).

### For California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request its deletion. This site does not sell personal information.

## Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this site, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.
